Stats & Probability

Understand statistical concepts like mean, median, standard deviation, and probability.

1. What are Statistics?

Statistics is the study of collecting, analyzing, interpreting, and presenting data. It is essential for making data-driven decisions.

2. Key Concepts

  • Mean: The average of a dataset.
  • Median: The middle value in a dataset.
  • Standard Deviation: Measures the spread of data.
  • Probability: The likelihood of an event occurring.

3. Examples

  • Calculate the mean and median of a dataset.
  • Compute the probability of rolling a six on a die.
