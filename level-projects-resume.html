Projects & Resume Prep

Build projects and prepare your resume to showcase your skills.

Why Build Projects?

Projects demonstrate your skills and problem-solving abilities to potential employers. They provide practical experience and help you stand out in the job market.

Step-by-Step Guide to Building Projects

  1. Choose a Project Idea: Select a project that aligns with your interests and the skills you want to showcase.
  2. Plan Your Project: Break down the project into smaller tasks and set a timeline for completion.
  3. Implement the Project: Write code, create visualizations, or build models based on your project idea.
  4. Test and Debug: Ensure your project works as expected and fix any issues.
  5. Document Your Work: Write a README file and document your code to make it easy for others to understand.
  6. Showcase Your Project: Add the project to your portfolio, GitHub, or resume to showcase your skills.
